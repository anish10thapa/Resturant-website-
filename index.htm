About 363

Born from passion

Our Story

The 363 story

363 Lifestyle Venue was founded in 2022 with one vision: to create a space in Itahari that rivals any world-class venue — in service, atmosphere, cuisine, and experience. We believed Eastern Nepal deserved better, and so we built it.

Our name isn't just a number. It represents our commitment — 363 exceptional experiences a year. Every night we open our doors is an opportunity to exceed expectations, whether you're celebrating a milestone or simply unwinding after a long day.

From day one, we invested in our team. Our chefs trained across India and Southeast Asia. Our bartenders studied in Kathmandu and internationally. The result is a level of craft and hospitality that Itahari had never seen before.

Today, 363 is more than a venue — it's a landmark. A place where birthdays are made magical, where first dates become love stories, where business dinners become partnerships, and where Friday nights become the stories people tell Monday morning.

What We Stand For

Our values

🎯
Uncompromising Quality
From the sourcing of every ingredient to the final plating — quality is non-negotiable. We work with only the finest local and imported produce.
🤝
Genuine Hospitality
Our team is trained to anticipate needs before they're expressed. Every guest is treated as a VIP, every visit as a privilege.
✨
Creative Innovation
Seasonal menus, rotating cocktail programs, and ever-evolving event concepts keep the 363 experience fresh and surprising.
🌿
Local Roots
We champion local farmers, producers, and artisans. Our menu is a love letter to Eastern Nepal's incredible culinary heritage.
🎵
Curated Atmosphere
Every detail — the lighting, the music, the scent, the seating — is intentionally designed to create the perfect emotional experience.
🏆
Community Pride
363 exists to elevate Itahari. We invest in our community, employ locally, and celebrate the culture that makes this city extraordinary.
